About Us

Dr. Malan Malumani does research in Dermatology and Venereology with an interest in cutaneous malignancies and dermatoses in retroviral disease. He is a bona fide graduate of Wuhan University, affiliated to Zhongnan Hospital; the Second Clinical College of Wuhan University.
He is able to treat a wide range of skin diseases like eczema, acne, Keloids, allergic/irritant contact dermatitis, drug rashes, skin cancer screen and treatment, viral and bacterial skin infection, fungal infections to mention just a few
